My dearest Kathija,

There are moments in life that arrive so softly, so quietly, that you almost don't notice them — and then you look back and realise they changed everything. Meeting you was one of those moments.

You are, in every sense, the most beautiful accident of my life. The way your laughter fills a room, the way your eyes hold entire worlds, the way you make ordinary days feel extraordinary — I never knew I was missing all of this until you.

I write this letter not because words are enough — they never will be — but because I want you to have something to hold onto. Something to return to on days when you forget how deeply, endlessly, fiercely loved you are.

You are my favourite person. My calm and my chaos. My home.

Every day I choose you. Every day I would choose you again.

Forever yours ♡
